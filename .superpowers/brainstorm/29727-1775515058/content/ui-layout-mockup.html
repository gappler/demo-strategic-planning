Key Interactions

  • Scenario switcher (sidebar) — Click to switch. Cards animate: shared objectives cross-fade with updated KR targets, scenario-specific cards slide in/out
  • Function filter (pill bar) — Toggle to show/hide functional areas
  • Card expand — Click any card to expand it, showing full KR detail, owner, timeline, and the strategic challenge it addresses
  • Objective toggle — Each card has a subtle on/off toggle. Disabling an objective grays it out and updates the sidebar resource summary in real-time
  • Resource summary (sidebar) — Always shows aggregate headcount and budget delta for the active scenario with current toggles
