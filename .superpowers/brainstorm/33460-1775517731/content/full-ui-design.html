Zone Summary

The page has 5 zones stacked vertically:

  1. Sticky header bar — Brand + live KPIs (Y3 revenue, margin, investment, headcount, risk) that update as you change anything
  2. Strategic move cards — Three toggle cards showing each move's impact summary. Click to enable/disable. Enabled cards are color-coded; disabled are grayed out.
  3. Slider panels — Only shows panels for enabled moves. Each has primary sliders + collapsible advanced settings. Disabled moves show a "enable above to configure" placeholder.
  4. Projection area — Stacked bar chart (12 quarters, color-coded by revenue source), margin trend, cumulative investment, headcount breakdown, and active risk factors.
  5. Pin bar + comparison drawer — Name and pin current config. Drawer collapses to show pinned scenario chips, expands to show full comparison table with all metrics side by side.
